Ingredients

  • 16oz of Pasta
  • 1 can of crushed tomatos
  • 1 eggplant

Instructions

  1. cut the eggplant into cubes and cook in the oven for 20 minutes.
  2. prepare the sauce in a preheated pan, with the tomatos and spices as preferred.
  3. add the eggplant to the sauce.
  4. cook the pasta.
  5. mix the sauce and pasta, then plate.

Done!
